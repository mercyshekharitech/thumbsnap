How to Use ThumbSnap

1

Paste YouTube Video URL

Go to YouTube, find the video whose thumbnail you want to download, and copy its URL from the browser's address bar. Then, paste this URL into the input field on our homepage.

2

Click "Get Thumbnails"

After pasting the URL, click the "Get Thumbnails" button. Our system will quickly process the link and fetch all available thumbnail resolutions for that video.

3

Choose and Download

You will see a variety of thumbnail sizes, from default to max resolution. Simply click the "Download" button next to your preferred thumbnail to save it directly to your device.

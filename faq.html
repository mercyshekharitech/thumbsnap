Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about ThumbSnap.

What is ThumbSnap?
ThumbSnap is a free online tool that allows you to easily download high-quality thumbnail images from any YouTube video by simply pasting its URL.
Is ThumbSnap free to use?
Yes, ThumbSnap is completely free to use. There are no hidden costs, subscriptions, or registration required.
Do I need to register or sign up?
No, you do not need to register or sign up to use ThumbSnap. It's designed for instant, hassle-free access.
What thumbnail resolutions are available?
ThumbSnap provides various resolutions, including MaxResDefault (1280x720), High Quality (480x360), Medium Quality (320x180), Standard Quality (640x480), and Default (120x90), among others.
How do I download a thumbnail? }
Simply paste the YouTube video URL into the input field on the homepage, click 'Get Thumbnails', and then select your desired resolution to download with a single click.
Is it legal to download YouTube thumbnails?
Downloading YouTube thumbnails for personal use or fair use purposes (e.g., review, commentary) is generally acceptable. However, using them for commercial purposes without permission from the content creator may infringe on copyright.
